Active Fire Map - Europe

The video below shows locations in Europe where fires have been detected on the earth's surface from satellites.

Each dot on the map represents a fire detected on a given day. The size of the dot represents the intensity of the fire.

This app is based on the EOCIS Active Fire Map and Fire Radiative Power dataset.

For more visualisations, information and data please visit the research group's site: https://wildfire.geog.kcl.ac.uk/products-and-data/
