Mean Surface Elevation Change (SEC) in Antarctica per year

This app is based on the EOCIS Antarctic SEC dataset. For more information, please see the user guide.

This dataset was produced by the Centre For Polar Observation and Modelling. For more information, please visit our homepage at https://www.cpom.org.uk/.

The plot below shows how the height of the Antarctic ice sheets changed over time from 1996 to the current day. Height changes are displayed for the whole ice sheet as well as individual drainage basins where ice flows from inland towards the ocean.

These ice sheet height time series are derived solely from recorded satellite measurements without any interpolation, which means they may underestimate the total height changes. This is because not all regions of the ice sheet are observed, particularly some of the fastest-changing areas, resulting in incomplete data coverage.
